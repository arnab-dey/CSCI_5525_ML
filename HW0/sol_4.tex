\subsection*{Solution 4}
The solution to 
\begin{align*}
	\max\limits_{w \in \mathbb{R}^n:w^Tw = 1}w^TAw
\end{align*}
is the largest eigen-value of $A$ and the solution to 
\begin{align*}
	\min\limits_{w \in \mathbb{R}^n:w^Tw = 1}w^TAw
\end{align*}
is the smallest eigen-value of $A$.
